Travel

First Pres is located at 2407 Dana St, Berkeley CA.  Paid parking is available in the following lots:
  1. First Pres parking garage on Channing St (hourly, $9 max)
  2. the Telegraph Channing garage (hourly, $15 max)
  3. the UC Ellsworth Lot (hourly, $15 max).  While street parking is also available in the surrounding area, we advise parking in the lots due to the concurrent Cal move-in day.  First Pres is also a 15 minute walk from the downtown Berkeley BART station.
